Why Choose Computer Science?

I have always excelled at problem solving. As a child, I taught myself how to use the family computer, in the small amount of screen time I was permitted daily. Quickly, I realized that performing any task on a computer was in itself an exercise in problem solving. Today, I have fallen in love with the logic found in modern coding languages. Within every project I build is a challenge, and I find translating human logic into a form understandable by a computer to be an extremely rewarding process.
